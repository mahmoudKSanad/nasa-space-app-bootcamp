Event Rules

Teams must consist of 2 to 6 members. All members must be registered for the event. We encourage diverse teams with a mix of skills, including developers, designers, scientists, and storytellers.

All work on the project must be done during the hackathon weekend. You may not use any code that was written before the event began. Use of open-source libraries and frameworks is permitted.

Your project must use at least one dataset from NASA's open data portal. The creative and effective use of this data will be a key judging criterion.

All participants must adhere to the event's Code of Conduct. We are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone. Any form of harassment or disrespect will not be tolerated.
